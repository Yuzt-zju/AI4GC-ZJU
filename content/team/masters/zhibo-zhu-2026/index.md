## About Me

Hi! I'm **Zhibo Zhu**, an incoming master's student at AI4GC Lab, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Shengyu Zhang. I will join the lab in September 2026.

My current research centers on **efficient video generation**, especially making video diffusion models faster and more practical at inference time. 

## Research Interests

- **Video Generation** - Developing efficient video generation methods that balance visual fidelity, temporal consistency, and computational cost.
- **Diffusion Models** - Studying diffusion-based generative models, especially the feature dynamics that emerge during iterative denoising.
- **VDM Inference Acceleration** - Accelerating video diffusion models through training-free caching, feature reuse, adaptive block skipping, and test-time self-calibration.
